Time Line

Robocon 2020

The theme for ABU Robocon 2020 was "ROBO RUGBY 7s," which was based on Fiji's national sport, rugby. The game was played between two teams, Red and Blue, using two robots each, one called a Pass Robot (PR) and the other a Try Robot (TR). The PR starts in the PR Start Zone, picks up a Try Ball from the Ball Rack, and passes it to the TR in the Receiving Zone. The game lasts a maximum of three minutes, and five obstacles act as five defending players. The main challenge of the game was the Goal Kick, which involved kicking the Kick Ball over the crossbar of the conversion post.

Robocon 2023

In 2023, the theme of the competition is to cast flowers over Angkor Wat by the corporation of a rabbit robot and an elephant robot. The actual competition is "Ring Toss Game' using blue and red rings made of rubber hoses instead of flowers.
